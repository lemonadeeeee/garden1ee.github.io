Welcome to Puddies!

Puddies is a social network service for dog owners. Let your dog make nearby friends and for happier dog life!


Our features

Recommend friends you would absolutely like

Our strong friend recommendation service brings you to lovely dogs in your neighborhood. You can simply fill out your profile and give your preferences to meet soulmate of your dog.

Join group meetups and have fun

Looking for frisbee friends? How about sharing hand-made snack recipes? In Puddies, you can find a variety of awesome meetup events. do activities together, and share useful tips. Or you can even create your own meetup event!

Be conneted, be safe!

In Puddies, you don't need to leave your contact info. With our strong built-in message service, you can be fully connected with your friends just standing here. Just take a try!
